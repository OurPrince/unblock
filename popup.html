AI Studio Unblock

v3.2.0
Active

What is this?

Removes the "Content Blocked" warning in AI Studio. The model's generated text stays visible instead of being replaced with an error.

How does it work?

Intercepts the response stream before the UI processes it:

1 Neutralizes block signals in the response
2 Prevents stream termination (abort block)
3 AI Studio shows the text as normal ✓

Good to know

Text may be truncated by the server. If that happens, just type "continue" to get the rest. Free & open source, no data collection.

Works only on aistudio.google.com
